About

Astrology, stargazing and space weather are my hobbies. I'm drawn to exploring unconventional branches of astrology.

My main areas of interests are western sidereal astrology, constellational (true sidereal) astrology, Mesopotamian astrology, and cosmobiology.

I was disappointed with popular Western astrology when I realized that its tropical zodiac is not related to the stars at all. I decided to work with sidereal zodiac (Fagan-Bradley), and constellations (13+ zodiacs). To me, the influence of the real stars is more probable than that of the tropical zodiac.

I don't ignore cosmic radiation, solar activity, and Earth weather as cosmic factors that affect humans as well.
