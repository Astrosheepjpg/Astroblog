About

I was disappointed with popular Western astrology when I realized that its tropical zodiac is not related to the stars at all.

I was literally asking: "Ok, but where are STARS?". To me, the influence of the real stars is more probable than that of the tropical zodiac.

Astrological research turned out to be a real rabbit hole. I found less popular branches of astrology and answers to some of my questions. 

For now, my main areas of interests are western sidereal astrology, constellational (true sidereal) astrology, Mesopotamian astrology, cosmobiology and fixed stars.

I decided to work with sidereal zodiac (Fagan-Bradley), and constellations (13+ zodiacs).
