Books and software

Books about astrology

 

Archive.org

Legal library with free books about any topic. Lots of high quality astrology texts! The catch? You need to sign up and read online, but there is no fees. Link: HERE

Academia.edu

Platform with free and paid academic articles about any topic, including astrology. Requires creation of an account. Link: HERE

 

Astrological software

 

Astro-Seek

Free website with astrological tools. There is almost everything. Good place to start, if you are a beginner. Link: HERE. For tropical birth chart: here. For sidereal birth chart: here.

PlanetDance

Free astrological software for Windows. It is at the level of professional software and allows advanced calculations. After downloading ephemeris it can work offline. For educational purposes there is useful option to generate short ready-made interpretations. Link: HERE

Astrology Master

The best free, mobile astrology app I've seen. Tropical and sidereal zodiac, many ayanamshas, additional objects to show (various points, hypothetical planets, asteroids, fixed stars, midpoints), natal birth charts and a few predictive and relationship charts. Links: ANDROID, iOS

Stellarium

Free (and super pretty!) astronomical  app for observing and recognizing stars in real time. Helpful for understanding the sky. It also has an option to set a date and location. You can check the true alignment of the constellations on the day of your birth. This can sometimes be a devastating experience and undermines everything you thought about astrology, including sidereal astrology based on 12 zodiacs. Links: DESKTOP, ANDROID, iOS
