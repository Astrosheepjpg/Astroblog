Western sidereal astrology

Intro

I’m an astrology enthusiast exploring western sidereal astrology after finding the tropical zodiac to be a disappointment - a beautiful lie that doesn’t align with the stars. I choose to use sidereal ("starry") zodiac. On my BLOG I share my journey and insights into this approach to astrology.

FAQ

What is western sidereal astrology? ￬ 

It uses sidereal zodiac with Western interpretation methods. Also incorporates unique techniques specific to this system.

What is the difference between tropical and sidereal zodiac? ￬ 

The tropical zodiac is a fixed calendar, while the sidereal shifts with the stars, changing the sign order.

Is sidereal zodiac synonymous with Vedic astrology? ￬ 

No, its roots are Babylonian. Vedic astrology adopted it later. Sidereal was also the first zodiac in ancient Greece and the original for all astrology. It can be used with western astrology.

What's the difference between western sidereal and vedic astrology? ￬ 

Western sidereal blends Western and Babylonian methods, while Vedic astrology follows its own techniques and meanings rooted in Vedic mythology.

What ayanamsha is typical for western sidereal? ￬ 

Fagan-Bradley.

What ayanamsha do you use? ￬ 

Galactic Center at 0° Sagittarius.

How many types of astrology exists? ￬ 

Western tropical, western sidereal, vedic sidereal and true sidereal (constellational).
